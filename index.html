The Wayne County Healthy Communities COVID-19 Testing Portal
Please register or login to an account to begin your easy COVID testing preocess.

Once logged in, you will be directed to select one of four options:
  1. Account Edits, where your account information can be viewed.
  2. Schedule, where you can schedule your appointment at your earliest convinience.
  3. Check-In, for call free appointment check-in!
  4. Results, the page to view all previous and current test results.
