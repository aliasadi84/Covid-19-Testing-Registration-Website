This is the Wayne County Healthy Communities COVID Testing Portal.
Please register or login to an account to begin your easy COVID testing process.
Once logged in, you will be able to select one of four options:

  1. Account, where your account information can be viewed and edited
  2. Schedule, where you can schedule your COVID test
  3. Check-In, where you can check in to your appointment
  4. Results, where you can view test results for all of your appointments
