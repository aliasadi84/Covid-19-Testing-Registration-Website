Welcome!

This is the Wayne Healthy Communities COVID-19 Testing Portal. As a patient, please login with the patient login button.

Once logged in, you will be directed to select one of four option:
  1. Account Edits
  2. Appointment Schedule
  3. Online Check-In
  4. View Test Results

Instructions

Your Account

At account you can edit your basic account information, such as name, email, and phone number.

Appointment Schedule

At appointment schedule you can schedule your COVID-19 test on an available day or time. You will then be asked to complete a questionnaire before your appointment will be scheduled.

Online Check-In

When you are ready to attend your appointment, please use our online check-in. This way our staff will be able to locate you with ease and quickly administer the test.

View Test Results

When your COVID test results are in, use the test results button to view your results.
