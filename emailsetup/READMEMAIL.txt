Enable Email functionality:
===========================
1st. Copy the information in phpconfig.txt found with in this folder
2nd. Go to C:\xampp\php and find php.ini (the configuration file) highlight everything then paste the information from phpcongif.txt
3rd. Copy the information in sendemailconfig.txt found with in this folder
4th. Go to C:\xampp\sendmail and find sendemail.ini (the configuration file) highlight everything then paste the information from sendemailconfig.txt
